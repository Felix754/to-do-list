Welcome to my To-Do List!

This tool is designed to help you stay organized and manage your tasks and projects efficiently. With a clean, user-friendly interface, the To-Do List allows you to create, view, and manage your projects and tasks all in one place. Here's a detailed overview of the features and functionality of my site.

Key Features

Project Management:

  • Create and organize multiple projects to categorize your tasks effectively.
  • Easily add and new projects with a simple input box and submit button.
  • View a list of all your projects and select a project to manage its own tasks.
  • Remove projects that are no longer needed by clicking on the close icon.

Task Management:

  • Add tasks to your selected projects with a straightforward input box and submit button.
  • View a list of all tasks associated with the selected project.
  • Mark tasks as complete by clicking on them.
  • Remove tasks that are no longer needed by clicking on the close icon.

User Interface:

  • A responsive and intuitive interface that adjusts to different screen sizes and devices.
  • Navigation icons for easy access to the menu and theme settings.
  • A clean and minimalistic design to enhance user experience.

      • Dark Mode:

      • Toggle between light and dark themes to suit your preferences.
      • The theme setting is saved locally, so your preference is retained even after you close and reopen the application.

      Local Storage:

      • All your projects and tasks are saved locally in your browser`s storage.
      • Your data persists across sessions, so you never lose track of your tasks and projects.

      Technical Details

      The application is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Projects and tasks are stored in the browser`s local storage, ensuring persistence across sessions. The interface adapts to both light and dark themes based on user preference. Semantic HTML elements are used to enhance the structure and accessibility of the application.
